# Materials and Methods

## Study Site
- **Location:** Abandoned arable field, Lesbos, Greece (39° N, 27° E).

## Experimental Design
- **Litterbag Experiment:** 220 litter bags (10 cm x 10 cm, nylon mesh with holes of 1.0 mm). Mass of ∼2 g of dried plant litter material inside. Spread within a 16 m^2 area.
- **Species:** 10 grassland species across 3 functional groups (grasses, legumes, non-leguminous forbs).
- **Duration:** 90 days (Autumn 2005).
- **Age of Ecosystem:** >15 years old
